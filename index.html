NAïVE Work

⇩

Adele Roncey and India Sachi co-founders of NAïVE.
Created in the 524 Homer Hall Dormroom, NAïVE hopes to create platform that will help artists meet others and share their work,
get the representation they need to prove that they are not just "NAïVE" young creatives.
NAïVE is a collective where there are no rules or guidelines for expression.
--> Artists showcase and promote their work on our plateform through design collaborations creating clothing and accessories sold to the public.
--> NAïVE has its own design collection in editions featuring cloting, accessories, bags, stickers, pins, all handmade.
